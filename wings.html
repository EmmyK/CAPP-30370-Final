Chicken Wings

Buffalo Chicken Wings

Recipe

Ingredients

  • raw chicken wings
  • Frank's RedHot Sauce
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp Tobasco
  • 1 tsp cajun seasoning mix of your choice

Instructions

  1. Grill chicken wings.
  2. While wings are cooking, place all other ingredients in large pot with lid. Turn heat on low, and let sauce melt together
  3. When wings are done cooking on the grill, place sings in sauce pot for minimum of 30 minutes. Serve with ranch dressing!
